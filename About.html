IU (아이유) is a South Korean solo singer and actress under EDAM Entertainment. She debuted on September 18, 2008 under Kakao M (formerly LOEN Entertainment).

Here a quick guide line about iu


Stage name: Iu

Birth Name: Lee Ji Eun

Nickname: Nations Little Sister and Nations SweetHeart

Birthday: May 16,1993

MBTI Type: INFJ

Timeline

1993

May 16: Born in Seoul, South Korea.

2008

Debut as a singer: IU debuts under LOEN Entertainment with the single "Lost Child" from her first extended play, Lost and Found. Though her initial releases were not commercially successful, she gained attention for her vocal ability.

2009

First breakthrough: The song "Boo" from her first studio album Growing Up starts to gain attention.

Rising popularity: "Marshmallow" from her second extended play IU...IM also performs well.

2010

National recognition: IU becomes widely recognized with the release of "Good Day," the lead single from her third extended play Real. The song tops charts and helps solidify her status in K-pop.

2011

Acting debut: IU stars in the teen drama Dream High, gaining recognition for her acting as well.

Chart dominance: Releases the hit single "You & I" from her second studio album Last Fantasy, which also tops various charts.

2013

Album Modern Times: Her third studio album Modern Times is a commercial success and showcases a more mature style in both music and lyrics.

Acting: Stars in You're the Best, Lee Soon-shin, her first leading role in a television drama.

2015

Album Chat-Shire: IU releases her fourth extended play Chat-Shire, receiving critical acclaim but also some controversy over certain lyrics.

Leading role in The Producers: IU stars in the popular drama The Producers, winning praise for her performance.

2017

Album Palette: IU releases her fourth studio album Palette, which marks a turning point in her musical style, combining K-pop with elements of indie pop. The title track, featuring G-Dragon, becomes a major hit, and the album wins several awards, including Album of the Year at the Melon Music Awards.

Acting in My Mister: IU stars in the drama My Mister, receiving widespread praise for her acting and winning numerous awards.

2019

Album Love Poem: IU releases her fifth extended play Love Poem, which is both a commercial and critical success.

Acting in Hotel Del Luna: IU stars in Hotel Del Luna, a supernatural drama that becomes a massive hit across Asia.

2020

Single "Eight": Collaborates with BTS’s Suga on the single "Eight," which tops charts globally.

Acting in Dream: IU begins filming for the movie Dream alongside actor Park Seo-joon.

2021

Album LILAC: IU releases her fifth studio album LILAC, exploring retro and pop sounds. The album is well received and solidifies her position as one of the top solo artists in South Korea.

Film debut in Broker: IU films her movie debut Broker, directed by Hirokazu Kore-eda.

2022

Continued success: IU continues to perform in various concerts and remains a top figure in both music and acting.

2023

Global recognition: IU’s status as one of the most influential figures in South Korea is reaffirmed through her continued chart-topping releases and major film roles.

2024

New Projects: IU remains active with upcoming musical releases and acting roles, cementing her reputation as a multi-talented star.
